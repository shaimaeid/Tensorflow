The Dataset¶

This data set contains 113,937 loans with 81 variables on each loan, including loan amount, borrower rate (or interest rate), current loan status, borrower income, and many others. After Data cleaning and removing unwanted variables we hae 16 variables and 82682 rows. in the following analysis I will examine the relation between loan status and other variables in an attampt to know which loans get paid and which has a potential risk of not getting paid.

Feature(s) of interest¶

Created,Term, Status,BorrowerRate,Yield, Loss, Return, Category, BorrowerState, Occupation, EmploymentStatus.

Exploratory Data Analysis¶

In this section I'm trying to answer the following questions:

  1. How many loans initiated per year?
  2. How many Past due loans?
  3. What duration is most frequent?
  4. Who got the loans?
  5. Who is not paying loans?

Conclusions¶

Using the dataset samples (82682 after cleaning) and during years (2009 : 2013 ) I found the following:

  • 2% of loans are Past-Due
  • Most loans were given for 36 months duration.
  • Most loans were given for employed borrowers.
  • Highest number of loans (42% of total loans)given was in 2013 and lowest in 2009 (2% of total loans)
  • 100% of past due loans were given to employed borrowers
